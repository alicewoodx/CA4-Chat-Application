More About Alice Wood...

I completed my teaching qualification in October 2021, and am now fully qualified. I joined Aberdeen Swimming Acadmey in November 2022, and have since been promoted to being a partner within the business. Being a partner envolves attending meetings to discuss the achievements the company has made and how these achievements could be improved. As a result of this qualification I can now demonstrate transferable skills such as leadership, adaptability, punctuality, patience, problem solving, ability to motivate and communicate effectively. Being a swimming teacher allows me to help swimmers and watch them progress. This is fulfilling as it proves that I am developing my teaching techniques.
